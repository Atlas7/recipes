Receipe for Bean on Toast - perfect for students on budget!

Preparation time: about 10 minutes! Serve 2.

Ingredients:

	- a can of baked beans.
	- 4 slices of bread.
	- spoonful of butter.

Directions:

	1 - Pop the bread into the toaster. Until crispy and golden in color..
	2 - In the meantime, open the can of baked beans, pour into a glass bowl, pop into the microwave for 3 minutes till pipping hot.
	3 - Spread a spoonful of butter to the toasted bread. Place on a plate.
	4 - Pour the hot baked beans all over the toasts!
	5 - Served hot!
